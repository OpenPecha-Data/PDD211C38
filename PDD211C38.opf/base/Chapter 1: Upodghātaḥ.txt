1. Upodghātaḥ

sarvadā sarvathā sarve yasya doṣā na santi ha |sarve sarvābhisāreṇa yatra cāvasthitā guṇāḥ ||
1

tam eva śaraṇaṃ gantuṃ taṃ stotuṃ tam upāsitum |tasyaiva śāsane sthātuṃ nyāyyaṃ yady asti cetanā
|| 2

savāsanāś ca te doṣā na santy ekasya tāyinaḥ |sarve sarvavidaḥ santi guṇās te cānapāyinaḥ ||
3

na hi pratiniviṣṭo ’pi manovākkāyakarmasu |saha dharmeṇa labhate kaś cid bhagavato ’ntaram ||
4

so ’haṃ prāpya manuṣyatvaṃ sasaddharmamahotsavam |mahārṇavayugacchidrakūrmagrīvārpaṇopamam ||
5

anityatāvyanusṛtāṃ karmacchidrasasaṃśayām |āttasārāṃ kariṣyāmi kathaṃ nemāṃ sarasvatīm || 6

ity asaṃkhyeyaviṣayān avetyāpi guṇān muneḥ |tadekadeśapraṇayaḥ kriyate svārthagauravāt || 7

svayaṃbhuve namas te ’stu prabhūtādbhutakarmaṇe |yasya saṃkhyāprabhāvābhyāṃ na guṇeṣv asti niścayaḥ
|| 8

iyanta iti nāsty anta īdṛśā iti kā kathā |puṇyā ity eva tu guṇān prati te mukharā vayam || 9
